# Ken Jones Photography

Ken Jones Photography is a New York City commercial photography and video production studio based on Fulton Street in Manhattan’s Financial District.

Led by photographer and video producer Ken Jones, the studio specializes in corporate headshots, executive portraits, fashion and beauty photography, editorial campaigns, product photography, Manhattan photo studio rental, and professional interview video production.

With more than 30 years of experience, Ken works with corporate clients, agencies, fashion brands, media companies, executives, entrepreneurs, and creative teams who need polished visual content created with professional lighting, direction, retouching, and production support.
